Cloudiness

​

Data on cloudiness of cities in relation to the equator presented interesting instances. There is a large a mount of cities within the first northern 40 degrees of the equator that have a 0% cloudiness level. Generally cities north of the equator have both a higher cloudiness percentage and a percentage at 0.
